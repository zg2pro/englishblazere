DVD @ Home

A library of movies

This application allows you to identify your movies

Thus, you can enter the title and a set of criteria on a movie. Among these, you will find:

  • -the title
  • -the title in English
  • -movie genres
  • -the country of origin of the movie
  • -the URL of the movie page on IMDB
  • -score of the movie on IMDB
  • -the score that you assign
  • -year movie distribution
  • -an opinion on sound quality
  • -an opinion on the film
  • -a section for additional comments (More. ..)

Note that you nup an option to automatically fill in missing data from the IMDB site. This feature is limited to a certain number of uses per hour.

Features

Creating an entry for a movie

To perform this action, you must be logged in as Administrator.

In the movie view, press the 'New' button.

Read a movie record

In the main view, open a movie link.

Edit a movie record

To perform this action, you must be logged in as Administrator.

In the movie view, press the 'Edit'(a pen) button in a row..

Delete a movie record

To perform this action, you must be logged in as Administrator.

In the movie view, press the 'Delete'(a red cross) button in a row or select any rows and press the 'Delete'(a red cross) in the toolbar.

Export data to CSV or SQL format

To perform this action, you must be logged in as Administrator.
